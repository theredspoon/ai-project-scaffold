# Phase Inbox

Completed phases copied from `TASKS.md` when they are removed through the Wrap Protocol.

Keep entries in completion order. Use the phase name as the entry heading, add a `Completed:` date, and replace `Expected Touches:` with `Actual Touches:` for the files or folders changed for reasons unrelated to the expected behavior of the tracking workflow. Keep entries here until they are consolidated into `MILESTONES.md` or otherwise explicitly resolved. If entries remain after milestone review, treat them as orphan inbox entries and report them in the completion response.
